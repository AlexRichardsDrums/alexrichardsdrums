I am Alex Richards.

A Drummer. Musician. & Educator.

I feel privileged to play a part in teaching drummers about their instrument and to help them achieve their personal goals. In my lessons I aim to provide students with the essential skills and technique needed to play confidently on the drum kit, whilst also underlining the importance of musicality, creativity, reading drum notation and developing their own unique sound and style.

I currently teach over 60 students a week across West Sussex and work freelance at Inclusive Music and Movement & Chichester Music Academy.

I have a BA (Hons) in Professional Musicianship- Drums, Grade 8 Drum Kit Certificate, and full DBS background check.

Please get in touch for more information. I look forward to hearing from you!
